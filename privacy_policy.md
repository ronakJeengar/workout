# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: June 14, 2026**

## 1. Data Collection

The Workout App is designed as an **offline-first** application. By default, all training data, including:

- Workout history
- Biometrics (Weight, Height)
- Training Programs
- Progress Analytics
is stored **locally on your device**.

## 2. Third-Party Analytics

If you opt-in to improve the app, anonymous usage data (e.g., "Session Completed") may be collected via our observability layer. No personally identifiable information (PII) is sent to external servers.

## 3. Data Control

- **Export:** You can export all your data in JSON format at any time.
- **Delete:** You can permanently delete all data via Settings.
- **Backup:** Local backups are unencrypted by default; use device-level encryption for added security.

## 4. Consent

By using this app, you agree to the local storage of your fitness data for the purpose of tracking and analytics.
